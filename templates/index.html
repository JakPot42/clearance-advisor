The 13 Adjudicative Guidelines

Security clearance adjudicators are trained to evaluate applicants against thirteen adjudicative guidelines established in Security Executive Agent Directive 4 (SEAD 4). No single guideline automatically disqualifies an applicant — adjudicators apply the Whole Person Concept, weighing context, recency, frequency, and demonstrated rehabilitation.
